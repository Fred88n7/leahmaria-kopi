Shipping & Returns

We ship world wide

Europe: 2/5 business days

USA: 10/15 business days

Leah Maria offers worldwide shipping on all orders. Your order will be shipped the day after its placed. Orders places during the weekend will be processed on the following Monday. Tracking information will be provided once the order is shipped. During holiday times your order might be shipped a day later than usual.

Non-execution of order

In case of non-execution of the order, you will receive a written notice and refund of any amount of money already paid.

Availability

The website will indicate the availability and unavailability of any products. If an order product is no longer available, the customer will be promptly refunded.

Defect products

The customer has three days from receiving the products to inform of any flaws. Slight differences and imperfections are to be considerd on handmade quality and unique products. If the product is considered defect the customers will need to provide a photographic prof. the product has to be in the original packaging.

Returns
