About Leah Maria

Leah Maria is a luxury concept store located in Charlottenlund north of Copenhagen. Entering Leah Maria is like opening the door to your dream closet. Everything is carefully hand-picked by owner Vicky Fogel and encapsulates the Leah Maria universe. In the store you will find a wide selection of Ready-to-Wear clothing, bags, shoes, accessories, fine jewelry and beauty products. The store also houses fine art and interior design. Leah Maria was founded in 2008 by Vicky Fogel who had a desire to provide an aesthetic, personal and inspirational shopping experience for her customers. In Leah Maria everyone is welcome, and our aim is to dress all customers and inspire them through a combination of high-end luxury goods and personal style in a relaxed and homely atmosphere. Enjoy our online platform that embodies our store, in hopes of capturing the spirit of our conscious and visionary universe.
